Types of Coffee

There are over 30 different types of making coffee, but an overview of the first five should be enough for the purpose of this page! (paragraph). The brown items do not contain milk.

  • Café Latte
  • Cappuccino
  • Espresso
  • Flat White
  • Long black
Coffee type Amount and type of milk
Café Latte A lot! No foam
Cappuccino Quite a lot, foamy
Espresso No milk
Flat White Less milk than a Latte
Long black No milk, much water though
